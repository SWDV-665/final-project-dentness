PHELogs - Info

Purpose

PHE Logs is a mobile app to expose Phenylalanine content of common foods. These measures are used by persons diagnosed with Phenylketonuria in measuring their daily allowance of protein.

For years, our family created a game out of memorizing these numbers. While it was interesting, inevitably, we would argue over who was right when there was a difference. The primary source of our data was a book that had PHE measurements listed much like a phone book. We have always wanted a mobile solution like PHELogs.
